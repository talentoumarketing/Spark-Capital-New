Our Story

Spark Capital was born out of our frustration as Angel Investors, having seen way too many startups perish at the infamous Death Valley curve. Our core teams extensive experience in bootstrapping and growing tech startups to multimillion revenue, led us to a new way to invest and incubate B2B tech startups.

It taught us the importance of execution over the ideas itself, the criticality of getting to paying customers quickly and the necessity to scale the startup beyond $1million ARR. Our investment model via Venture Studio is based on these learning and our early success with some of our startups getting to multimillion in annuity revenues.

Our Funds

Our capital strategy is built to confront the hardest phase of startup risk head-on. Fund 1 absorbs early uncertainty through ownership, control, and deep validation inside our Venture Studio. Fund 2 enters once the path forward is clear, revenue is established, and growth becomes repeatable. This structure allows risk to fall before capital scales.

Open to LPs

Fund 2

Having seen success with Fund 1, we are now on our 2nd Fund and continuing to onboard Limited Partners or LPs to Fund 2. With Fund 2, our aim is to enable LPs to participate in startup investing after early validation and revenue milestones are achieved. This means, Fund 2 comes in as growth capital for the startups and also the transition to a new leader for the next phase of the startups growth. Fund 2 takes 20 to 30% equity, with 10 to 20% allocated to incoming leadership, resulting in a planned and transparent dilution of Fund 1.

Fund II follows an LP-first structure where investor capital is returned in full, followed by a 7‚Äì8% preferred return, before profits are shared under a standard 80/20 carry. Investing only in post-revenue enterprise AI companies, the fund lowers risk while offering predictable cash flows, clear exits, and strong, risk-adjusted upside.
