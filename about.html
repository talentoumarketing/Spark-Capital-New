About Spark Capital

Building the next generation of enterprise AI companies

Our Story

Spark Capital was born out of our frustration as Angel Investors, having seen way too many startups perish at the infamous Death Valley curve. Our core team's extensive experience in bootstrapping and growing tech startups to multimillion revenue, led us to a new way to invest and incubate B2B tech startups.

It taught us the importance of execution over the ideas itself, the criticality of getting to paying customers quickly and the necessity to scale the startup beyond $1 million ARR. Our investment model via Venture Studio is based on these learnings and our early success with some of our startups getting to multimillion in annuity revenues.

Our core team consists of industry leaders from top-tier technology and venture capital companies, with experience in bootstrapping and growing tech startups to multimillion revenue. We are US market focused, with our headquarters in Tampa, Florida; and have global offices in London, UK and Chennai, India.

Our Unique 3 Phase Startup Investing Model

Our investing model takes a startup from ideation to MVP to revenue and beyond. We do this through a unique 3 phase startup investing model.

Phase 1 - Pre-seed via Fund 1

Fund 1 owns 100% of new startups and builds from idea to $1M ARR

Phase 2 - Seed via Fund 2

Fund 2 takes 20-30% equity with leadership transition

Phase 3 - Series A External

External Investors enter the cap table
